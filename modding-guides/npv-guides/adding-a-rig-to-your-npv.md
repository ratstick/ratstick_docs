# Adding a rig to your NPV

In the mod asset browser, find the rig you want to use and add the animgraph and rig files.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (3).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Next, open up your NPV's .ent and find the "components" section.

Expand it and look for "deformations". This is where you'll enter the path to the animgraph and rig. Here's where I set the Lethal Curves rig I use for Bea.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/2 (3).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

And that's it! Save your .ent and you're all set. Don't forget to update the photomode files too!
